Chapter 145

David's psalm of praise.

1)I will extol thee, my God, O kingKing; and I will bless thy name for everforever and ever.

2)Every day will I bless thee; and I will praise thy name for everforever and ever.

3)Great is the LORDLord, and greatly to be praised; and his greatness is unsearchable.

4)One generation shall praise thy works to another, and shall declare thy mighty acts.

5)I will speak of the glorious honourhonor of thy majesty, and of thy wondrous works.

6)And men shall speak of the might of thy terrible acts:; and I will declare thy greatness.

7)They shall abundantly utter the memory of thy great goodness, and shall sing of thy righteousness.

8)The LORDLord is gracious, and full of compassion; slow to anger, and of great mercy.

9)The LORDLord is good to all:; and his tender mercies are over all his works.

10)All thy works shall praise thee, O LORDLord; and thy saints shall bless thee.

11)They shall speak of the glory of thy kingdom, and talk of thy power;

12)To make known to the sons of men his mighty acts, and the glorious majesty of his kingdom.

13)Thy kingdom is an everlasting kingdom, and thy dominion endureth throughout all generations.

14)The LORDLord upholdeth all that fall, and raiseth up all those that be bowed down.

15)The eyes of all wait upon thee; and thou givest them their meat in due season.

16)Thou openest thine hand, and satisfiest the desire of every living thing.

17)The LORDLord is righteous in all his ways, and holy in all his works.

18)The LORDLord is nigh unto all them that call upon him, to all that call upon him in truth.

19)He will fulfilfulfill the desire of them that fear him:; he also will hear their cry, and will save them.

20)The LORDLord preserveth all them that love him:; but all the wicked will he destroy.

21)My mouth shall speak the praise of the LORD:Lord; and let all flesh bless his holy name for everforever and ever.
