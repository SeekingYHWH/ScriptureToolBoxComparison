Chapter 135

An exhortation to praise God -- The vanity of idols.

1)Praise ye the LORDLord. Praise ye the name of the LORDLord; praise him, O ye servants of the LORDLord.

2)Ye that stand in the house of the LORDLord, in the courts of the house of our God.,

3)Praise the LORDLord; for the LORDLord is good:; sing praises unto his name; for it is pleasant.

4)For the LORDLord hath chosen Jacob unto himself, and Israel for his peculiar treasure.

5)For I know that the LORDLord is great, and that our Lord is above all gods.

6)Whatsoever the LORDLord pleased, that did he in heaven, and in earth, in the seas, and all deep places.

7)He causeth the vapoursvapors to ascend from the ends of the earth; he maketh lightnings for the rain; he bringeth the wind out of his treasuries.

8)Who smote the firstbornfirst-born of Egypt, both of man and beast.

9)Who sent tokens and wonders into the midst of thee, O Egypt, upon Pharaoh, and upon all his servants.

10)Who smote great nations, and slew mighty kings;

11)Sihon king of the Amorites, and Og king of Bashan, and all the kingdoms of Canaan:;

12)And gave their land for ana heritage, ana heritage unto Israel his people.

13)Thy name, O LORDLord, endureth for everforever; and thy memorial, O LORDLord, throughout all generations.

14)For the LORDLord will judge his people, and he will not repent himself concerning his servants.

15)The idols of the heathen are silver and gold, the work of men's hands.

16)They have mouths, but they speak not; eyes have they, but they see not;

17)They have ears, but they hear not; neither is there any breath in their mouths.

18)They that make them are like unto them:; so is every oneeveryone that trusteth in them.

19)Bless the LORDLord, O house of Israel:; bless the LORDLord, O house of Aaron:;

20)Bless the LORDLord, O house of Levi:; ye that fear the LORDLord, bless the LORDLord.

21)Blessed be the LORDLord out of Zion,; whichBlessed dwellethbe atthe Lord out of Jerusalem. Praise ye the LORDLord.
