Chapter 27

David sustaineth his faith by prayer. (A Psalm of David.)

1)The LORDLord is my light and my salvation; whom shall I fear? the LORDLord is the strength of my life; of whom shall I be afraid?

2)When the wicked, even mine enemies and my foes, came upon me to eat up my flesh, they stumbled and fell.

3)Though ana host should encamp against me, my heart shall not fear:; though war should rise against me, in this will I beam confident.

4)One thing have I desired of the LORDLord, that will I seek after; that I may dwell in the house of the LORDLord all the days of my life, to behold the beauty of the LORDLord, and to enquireinquire in his temple.

5)For in the time of trouble he shall hide me in his pavilion:; in the secret of his tabernacle shall he hide me; he shall set me up upon a rock.

6)And now shall mine head be lifted up above mine enemies round about me:; therefore will I offer in his tabernacle sacrifices of joy; I will sing, yea, I will sing praises unto the LORDLord.

7)Hear, O LORDLord, when I cry with my voice:; have mercy also upon me, and answer me.

8)When thou saidst, Seek ye my face; my heart said unto thee, Thy face, LORDLord, will I seek.

9)Hide not thy face far from me; put not thy servant away in anger:; thou hast been my help; leave me not, neither forsake me, O God of my salvation.

10)When my father and my mother forsake me, then the LORDLord will take me up.

11)Teach me thy way, O LORDLord, and lead me in a plain path, because of mine enemies.

12)Deliver me not over unto the will of mine enemies:; for false witnesses are risen up against me, and such as breathe out cruelty.

13)I had fainted, unlessUnless I had believed to see the goodness of the LORDLord in the land of the living, thou wouldst deliver my soul into hell.

14)Thou didst say unto me, Wait on the LORD:Lord, be of good courage, and he shall strengthen thinethy heart:; wait, I say, on the LORDLord.
