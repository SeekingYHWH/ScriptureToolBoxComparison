Chapter 23

The feasts of the Lord -- The sabbath -- Gleanings to be left -- Theday of atonement.

1)And the LORDLord spake unto Moses, saying,

2)Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, Concerning the feasts of the LORDLord, which ye shall proclaim to be holy convocations, even these are my feasts.

3)Six days shall work be done:; but the seventh day is the sabbath of rest, ana holy convocation; ye shall do no work therein:; it is the sabbath of the LORDLord in all your dwellings.

4)These are the feasts of the LORDLord, even holy convocations, which ye shall proclaim in their seasons.

5)In the fourteenth day of the first month at even is the LORD'sLord's passover.

6)And on the fifteenth day of the same month is the feast of unleavened bread unto the LORD:Lord; seven days ye must eat unleavened bread.

7)In the first day ye shall have ana holy convocation:; ye shall do no servile work therein.

8)But ye shall offer an offering made by fire unto the LORDLord seven days:; in the seventh day is ana holy convocation:; ye shall do no servile work therein.

9)And the LORDLord spake unto Moses, saying,

10)Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, When ye be come into the land which I give unto you, and shall reap the harvest thereof, then ye shall bring a sheaf of the firstfruitsfirst-fruits of your harvest unto the priest:;

11)And he shall wave the sheaf before the LORDLord, to be accepted for you:; on the morrow after the sabbath the priest shall wave it.

12)And ye shall offer that day when ye wave the sheaf ana he lamb without blemish of the first year for a burnt offering unto the LORDLord.

13)And the meat offering thereof shall be two tenth deals of fine flour mingled with oil, anand offering made by fire unto the LORDLord for a sweet savor:; and the drink offering thereof shall be of wine, the fourth part of ana hin.

14)And ye shall eat neither bread, nor parched corn, nor green ears, until the selfsameself-same day that ye have brought an offering unto your God:; it shall be a statute for ever throughout your generations in all your dwellings.

15)And ye shall count unto you from the morrow after the sabbath, from the day that ye brought the sheaf of the wave offering; seven sabbaths shall be complete:;

16)Even unto the morrow after the seventh sabbath shall ye number fifty days; and ye shall offer a new meat offering unto the LORDLord.

17)Ye shall bring out of your habitations two wave loaves of two tenth deals; they shall be of fine flour; they shall be bakedbaken with leaven; they are the firstfruitsfirst-fruits unto the LORDLord.

18)And ye shall offer with the bread seven lambs without blemish of the first year, and one young bullock, and two rams:; they shall be for a burnt offering unto the LORDLord, with their meat offering, and their drink offerings, even an offering made by fire, of sweet savor unto the LORDLord.

19)Then ye shall sacrifice one kid of the goats for a sin offering, and two lambs of the first year for a sacrifice of peace offerings.

20)And the priest shall wave them with the bread of the firstfruitsfirst-fruits for a wave offering before the LORDLord, with the two lambs:; they shall be holy to the LORDLord for the priest.

21)And ye shall proclaim on the selfsameself-same day, that it may be ana holy convocation unto you:; ye shall do no servile work therein:; it shall be a statute for ever in all your dwellings throughout your generations.

22)And when ye reap the harvest of your land, thou shalt not make clean riddance of the corners of thy field when thou reapest, neither shalt thou gather any gleaning of thy harvest:; thou shalt leave them unto the poor, and to the stranger:; I am the LORDLord your God.

23)And the LORDLord spake unto Moses, saying,

24)Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, In the seventh month, in the first day of the month, shall ye have a sabbath, a memorial of blowing of trumpets, ana holy convocation.

25)Ye shall do no servile work therein:; but ye shall offer an offering made by fire unto the LORDLord.

26)And the LORDLord spake unto Moses, saying,

27)Also on the tenth day of this seventh month there shall be a day of atonement:; it shall be ana holy convocation unto you; and ye shall afflict your souls, and offer an offering made by fire unto the LORDLord.

28)And ye shall do no work in that same day:; for it is a day of atonement, to make an atonement for you before the LORDLord your God.

29)For whatsoever soul it be that shall not be afflicted in that same day, he shall be cut off from among his people.

30)And whatsoever soul it be that doeth any work in that same day, the same soul will I destroy from among his people.

31)Ye shall do no manner of work:; it shall be a statute for ever throughout your generations in all your dwellings.

32)It shall be unto you a sabbath of rest, and ye shall afflict your souls:; in the ninth day of the month at even, from even unto even, shall ye celebrate your sabbath.

33)And the LORDLord spake unto Moses, saying,

34)Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, The fifteenth day of this seventh month shall be the feast of tabernacles for seven days unto the LORDLord.

35)On the first day shall be ana holy convocation:; ye shall do no servile work therein.

36)Seven days ye shall offer an offering made by fire unto the LORD:Lord; on the eighth day shall be ana holy convocation unto you;, and ye shall offer an offering made by fire unto the LORD:Lord; it is a solemn assembly; and ye shall do no servile work therein.

37)These are the feasts of the LORDLord, which ye shall proclaim to be holy convocations, to offer an offering made by fire unto the LORDLord, a burnt offering, and a meat offering, a sacrifice, and drink offerings, every thingeverything upon his day:;

38)Beside the sabbaths of the LORDLord, and beside your gifts, and beside all your vows, and beside all your freewillfree-will offerings, which ye give unto the LORDLord.

39)Also in the fifteenth day of the seventh month, when ye have gathered in the fruit of the land, ye shall keep a feast unto the LORDLord seven days:; on the first day shall be a sabbath, and on the eighth day shall be a sabbath.

40)And ye shall take you on the first day the boughs of goodly trees, branches of palm -trees, and the boughs of thick trees, and willows of the brook; and ye shall rejoice before the LORDLord your God seven days.

41)And ye shall keep it a feast unto the LORDLord seven days in the year.; Itit shall be a statute for ever in your generations:; ye shall celebrate it in the seventh month.

42)Ye shall dwell in booths seven days; all that are Israelites born shall dwell in booths:;

43)That your generations may know that I made the children of Israel to dwell in booths, when I brought them out of the land of Egypt:; I am the LORDLord your God.

44)And Moses declared unto the children of Israel the feasts of the LORDLord.
