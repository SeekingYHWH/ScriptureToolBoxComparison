Chapter 23

Pilate sends Jesus to Herod -- He is scourged and sent back -- Scattering of Israel foretold -- Christ crucified -- Penitent thief -- Jesus buried by Joseph of Arimathea.

1)And the whole multitude of them arose, and led him unto Pilate.

2)And they began to accuse him, saying, We found this fellowman perverting the nation, and forbidding to give tribute to Caesar, saying, that he himself is Christ, a Kingking.
