The glory of Zion and Jerusalem.

32)And it shall come to pass, that hethey that isare left in Zion, and he that remaineth in Jerusalem, shall be called holy, even every oneeveryone that is written among the living in Jerusalem:;

54)And the LORDLord will create upon every dwelling place of mount Zion, and upon her assemblies, a cloud and smoke by day, and the shiningshinning of a flaming fire by night:; for upon all the glory of Zion shall be a defencedefense.
