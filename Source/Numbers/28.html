Chapter 28

Offerings to be observed.

1)And the LORDLord spake unto Moses, saying,

2)Command the children of Israel, and say unto them, My offering, and my bread for my sacrifices made by fire, for a sweet savor unto me, shall ye observe to offer unto me in their due season.

3)And thou shalt say unto them, This is the offering made by fire which ye shall offer unto the LORDLord; two lambs of the first year without spot day by day, for a continual burnt offering.

4)The one lamb shalt thou offer in the morning, and the other lamb shalt thou offer at even;

5)And a tenth part of an ephah of flour for a meat offering, mingled with the fourth part of ana hin of beaten oil.

6)It is a continual burntburn offering, which was ordained in mount Sinai for a sweet savor, a sacrifice made by fire unto the LORDLord.

7)And the drink offering thereof shall be the fourth part of ana hin for the one lamb:; in the holy place shalt thou cause the strong wine to be poured unto the LORDLord for a drink offering.

8)And the other lamb shalt thou offer at even:; as the meat offering of the morning, and as the drink offering thereof, thou shalt offer it, a sacrifice made by fire, of a sweet savor unto the LORDLord.

9)And on the sabbath day two lambs of the first year without spot, and two tenth deals of flour for a meat offering, mingled with oil, and the drink offering thereof:;

10)This is the burnt offering of every sabbath, beside the continual burnt offering, and his drink offering.

11)And in the beginnings of your months ye shall offer a burnt offering unto the LORDLord; two young bullocks, and one ram, seven lambs of the first year without spot;

12)And three tenth deals of flour for a meat offering, mingled with oil, for one bullock; and two tenth deals of flour for a meat offering, mingled with oil, for one ram;

13)And a several tenth deal of flour mingled with oil for a meat offering unto one lamb; for a burnt offering of a sweet savor, a sacrifice made by fire unto the LORDLord.

14)And their drink offerings shall be half ana hin of wine unto a bullock, and the third part of ana hin unto a ram, and a fourthforth part of ana hin unto a lamb:; this is the burnt offering of every month throughout the months of the year.

15)And one kid of the goats for a sin offering unto the LORDLord shall be offered, beside the continual burnt offering, and his drink offering.

16)And in the fourteenth day of the first month is the passover of the LORDLord.

17)And in the fifteenth day of this month is the feast:; seven days shall unleavened bread be eaten.

18)In the first day shall be ana holy convocation; ye shall do no manner of servile work therein:;

19)But ye shall offer a sacrifice made by fire for a burnt offering unto the LORDLord; two young bullocks, and one ram, and seven lambs of the first year:; they shall be unto you without blemish:.

20)And their meat offering shall be of flour mingled with oil:; three tenth deals shall ye offer for a bullock, and two tenth deals for a ram;

21)A several tenth deal shalt thou offer for every lamb, throughout the seven lambs:;

22)And one goat for a sin offering, to make an atonement for you.

23)Ye shall offer these beside the burnt offering in the morning, which is for a continual burnt offering.

24)After this manner ye shall offer daily, throughout the seven days, the meat of the sacrifice made by fire, of a sweet savor unto the LORD:Lord; it shall be offered beside the continual burnt offering, and his drink offering.

25)And on the seventh day ye shall have ana holy convocation; ye shall do no servile work.

26)Also in the day of the firstfruits, when ye bring a new meat offering unto the LORDLord, after your weeks be out, ye shall have ana holy convocation; ye shall do no servile work:

27)But ye shall offer the burnt offering for a sweet savor unto the LORDLord; two young bullocks, one ram, seven lambs of the first year;

28)And their meat offering of flour mingled with oil, three tenth deals unto one bullock, two tenth deals unto one ram,

29)A several tenth deal unto one lamb, throughout the seven lambs;

30)And one kid of the goats, to make an atonement for you.

31)Ye shall offer them beside the continual burnt offering, and his meat offering, (they shall be unto you without blemish,) and their drink offerings.
