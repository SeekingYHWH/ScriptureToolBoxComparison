Chapter 6

David fetcheth the ark from Kirjathjearim -- Uzzah is smitten -- David bringing the ark into Zion -- He placeth it in a tabernacle.

1)Again, David gathered together all the chosen men of Israel, thirty thousand.

2)And David arose, and went with all the people that were with him from Baale of Judah, to bring up from thence the ark of God, whose name is called by the name of the LORDLord of hosts that dwelleth between the cherubim.

3)And they set the ark of God upon a new cart, and brought it out of the house of Abinadab that was in Gibeah:; and Uzzah and Ahio, the sons of Abinadab, drave the new cart.

4)And they brought it out of the house of Abinadab which was at Gibeah, accompanying the ark of God:; and Ahio went before the ark.

5)And David and all the house of Israel played before the LORDLord on all manner of instruments made of fir wood, even on harps, and on psalteries, and on timbrels, and on cornets, and on cymbals.

6)And when they came to Nachon's threshingfloorthreshing floor, Uzzah put forth his hand to the ark of God, and took hold of it; for the oxen shook it.

7)And the anger of the LORDLord was kindled against Uzzah;, and God smote him there for his error; and there he died by the ark of God.

8)And David was displeased, because the LORDLord had made a breach upon Uzzah:; and he called the name of the place PerezuzzahPerez-uzzah to this day.

9)And David was afraid of the LORDLord that day, and said, How shall the ark of the LORDLord come to me?

10)So David would not remove the ark of the LORDLord unto him into the city of David:; but David carried it aside into the house of ObededomObed-edom the Gittite.

11)And the ark of the LORDLord continued in the house of ObededomObed-edom the Gittite three months:; and the LORDLord blessed ObededomObed-edom, and all his household.

12)And it was told king David, saying, The LORDLord hath blessed the house of ObededomObed-edom, and all that pertaineth unto him, because of the ark of God. So David went and brought up the ark of God from the house of ObededomObed-edom into the city of David with gladness.

13)And it was so, that when they that bare the ark of the LORDLord had gone six paces, he sacrificed oxen and fatlings.

14)And David danced before the LORDLord with all his might; and David was girded with a linen ephod.

15)So David and all the house of Israel brought up the ark of the LORDLord with shouting, and with the sound of the trumpet.

16)And as the ark of the LORDLord came into the city of David, Michal Saul's daughter looked through a window, and saw king David leaping and dancing before the LORDLord; and she despised him in her heart.

17)And they brought in the ark of the LORDLord, and set it in his place, in the midst of the tabernacle that David had pitched for it:; and David offered burnt offerings and peace offerings before the LORDLord.

18)And as soon as David had made an end of offering burnt offeringsoffering and peace offerings, he blessed the people in the name of the LORDLord of hosts.

19)And he dealt among all the people, even among the whole multitude of Israel, as well to the women as men, to every oneeveryone a cake of bread, and a good piece of flesh, and a flagon of wine. So all the people departed every oneeveryone to his house.

20)Then David returned to bless his household. And Michal the daughter of Saul came out to meet David, and said, How glorious was the king of Israel to daytoday, who uncovered himself to daytoday in the eyes of the handmaids of his servants, as one of the vain fellows shamelessly uncovereth himself!

21)And David said unto Michal, It was before the LORDLord, which chose me before thy father, and before all his house, to appoint me ruler over the people of the LORDLord, over Israel:; therefore will I play before the LORD.Lord;

22)And I will yet be more vile than thus, and will be base in mine own sight:; and of the maidservants which thou hasthas spoken of, of them shall I be had in honor.

23)Therefore Michal the daughter of Saul had no child unto the day of her death.
